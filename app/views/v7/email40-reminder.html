As the principal accountable person for Chester House, you have 6 days left to apply for your building assessment certificate by the Building Safety Regulator (BSR).

You must submit your application online at: www.gov.uk/guidance/apply-for-building-assessment-certification.

Find out what must be included in your application at: www.gov.uk/guidance/applying-for-building-assessment-certification.

If you do not apply for your building assessment certificate

Failure to submit your application within 28 days of being directed to apply is a criminal offence, under section 79(3) of the Building Safety Act 2022. BSR may consider taking enforcement action against you. Any enforcement action will be considered in line with our enforcement policy statement: URL.

If you are not able to submit your application for your building assessment certificate within 28 days of being directed to, then contact BSR to tell us the reasons why at: www.contact-building-safety-regulator.service.gov.uk.

You will need to pay

There is an application charge of £288, this can be paid by card or invoice.

The assessment of your application will also incur further ongoing charges. You will need to provide details so we can send you invoices for the work undertaken to assess your application. If you want to include a purchase order number on the invoices, you may want to get this before starting your application.
