Charges for assessing your application

There is an application charge of £288 for your building assessment certificate application. You can pay this by card or invoice.

The assessment of your application will also incur further ongoing charges. BSR will charge you:

  • £144 per person for every hour we spend assessing your application
  • for time spent by other relevant authorities or third parties assessing your application

We will send a monthly invoice by email for these charges.
