Confirm the relevant people are being provided with information

As part of your building assessment certificate application for Chester House, you are required as the principal accountable person to confirm that all accountable persons (APs) for Chester House have met the following duties. You will need to liaise with all APs to ensure that they are aware of what they need to do and have complied.

You and any other APs for Chester House are required to give information to:

  • the Building Safety Regulator
  • any other accountable persons for the building
  • residents of the building
  • owners of the residential units in the building
  • any other persons including a relevant landlord, client, relevant responsible person, fire & rescue authority

You are required to give them information on:

  • change of AP
  • resident engagement
  • requests for further information

If you need to, you can find out what information APs need to give to the persons listed above (opens in a new tab).

Every AP for Chester House has the same duty to give this information.

As the PAP you are required as part of the BAC application to make a statement confirming that you and any other APs for Chester House have given the required information to all persons as listed above.
