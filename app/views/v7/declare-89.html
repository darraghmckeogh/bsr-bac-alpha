Confirm the relevant people are being provided with information

The accountable person (AP) for a higher-risk building must create and maintain documents for the building, or part of the building, that they are responsible for. If the AP is also the principal accountable person (PAP), there are additional documents they must keep.

As part of your building assessment certificate application for Chester House, you are required as the PAP to confirm that all APs for Chester House have met the following duties. You will need to liaise with all APs to ensure that they are aware of what they need to do and have complied.

You and any other APs for Chester House are required to give information to:

  • the Building Safety Regulator
  • any other APs for the building
  • residents of the building
  • owners of the residential units in the building
  • any other persons including a relevant landlord, client, relevant responsible person, fire and rescue authority, on change of AP, resident engagement: requests for further information

If you need to, you can find out what information APs need to give to the persons listed above (opens in a new tab).

Every AP for Chester House has the same duty to give this information.

As the PAP you are required as part of the building assessment certificate application to make a statement confirming that you and any other APs for Chester House have given the required information to all persons as listed above.

By continuing:

[Greenways Limited] confirms that each accountable person has complied with their obligations to provide the information listed in the Higher-Risk Buildings (Keeping and Provision of Information etc.) (England) Regulations 2024 and The Higher-Risk Buildings (Management of Safety Risks etc.) Regulations 2023 to the persons listed above in accordance with section 89 of the Building Safety Act 2022.

[Greenways Limited] also confirms that [Greenways Limited] have notified the Building Safety Regulator of any change to Key Building Information within 28 days of [Greenways Limited] becoming aware of the change, in accordance with regulation 21 of the Higher-Risk Buildings (Key Building Information etc) (England) Regulations 2023.
